Our Story

Excellence in Egyptian Agricultural Exports

Emdad Global has been at the forefront of Egyptian agricultural exports for over 25 years. We specialize in premium quality citrus fruits, fresh fruits, vegetables, and frozen products that meet the highest international standards.

Our commitment to quality, combined with state-of-the-art facilities and comprehensive certifications, has made us a trusted partner for importers worldwide. We serve over 50 countries across Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas.

From our headquarters in Egypt, we coordinate with carefully selected farms and processing facilities to ensure that every product bearing the Emdad Global name meets our exacting standards for quality, freshness, and safety.
