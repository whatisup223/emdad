Shipping Terms & Options

Flexible terms to meet your business needs

FOB (Free on Board)

Goods delivered to the port of shipment. You arrange and pay for international shipping, insurance, and import duties.

Includes:
  • Product cost
  • Local transportation to port
  • Export documentation
  • Loading charges
Best for experienced importers

CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight)

Complete delivery to your destination port including shipping and insurance. You handle import duties and local delivery.

Includes:
  • All FOB services
  • International shipping
  • Marine insurance
  • Destination port charges
Recommended for new importers
